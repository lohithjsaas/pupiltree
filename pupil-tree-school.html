Academic Programs

A Curriculum for Every Learner

At Pupil Tree, we offer diverse academic pathways tailored to every child's unique potential and aspirations. Whether you seek a globally respected curriculum, a nationally recognized board, or a regionally rooted education system - we
provide the right fit for your learning journey.

ICSE
CBSE
SSLC

The ICSE curriculum nurtures analytical thinking, linguistic fluency, and strong foundations across sciences andhumanities. It emphasizes deep learning, creativity, and real-world application - ideal for students who thrive in anEnglish-medium academic environment.

Why Choose ICSE?

  • Strong focus on English, Science, and Mathematics
  • Emphasis on practical learning and project work
  • Balanced academic and co-curricular development
  • Encourages independent thinking and problem-solving

CBSE offers a well-structured, uniform curriculum designed to build conceptual clarity and practical understanding. Its streamlined approach supports progressive learning and skill development across all disciplines.

Why Choose CBSE?

  • Consistent and structured syllabus across India
  • Focus on conceptual understanding and applied learning
  • Wide acceptance in schools and colleges across the country
  • Activity-based learning and regular assessments

SSLC offers a curriculum that is contextually relevant and closely aligned with regional values and languages. It supports a strong academic foundation and helps students stay connected to their cultural identity.

Why Choose SSLC?

  • Familiar language and locally relevant content
  • Strong foundation in core subjects and value education
  • Encourages cultural awareness and community connection
  • Recognized by state-level institutions and colleges

  • Compassionate mentoring and continuous progress tracking
  • A strong focus on confidence, creativity, and critical thinking

Teaching & Learning

Pupil Tree strongly believes in ‘learning everywhere’, which does not confine to just classrooms. We constantly upgrade our curriculum, upskill and train the teachers on the global best practices and teaching methodologies to think beyond traditional teaching methodologies like rote and chalk and talk. This enables teachers to make the class engaging and interactive. Thus, giving an opportunity for every child to learn and understand the concept taught in the way she/he best understand. This child-centric, collaborative approach and methodologies in teaching-learning make our children imaginative, curious and confident Mentors and Caregivers

Our Teachers -dream Team

Some of the most dedicated, skilled, passionate and caring teachers work at Pupil Tree. They are more ‘mother/father’ at heart first and teachers next. This is the core quality that makes the heart beat at Pupil Tree. Every teachers wants to make a difference and ensure that ‘no child is left behind’. Apart from this what differentiates Pupil Tree Teachers from the rest is the positive energy, professionalism, always ready for change mindset, ready to experiment with new ideas and never give up attitude to continuously improve. This is the core work culture of the school.

This attitude makes our mentors a ‘Dream Team’

Beyond Academics

The Art and Cultural department plays a pivotal role in striking a fine balance between curricular and co-curricular education. The department offers an array of co-curricular activities like Indian and western classical music, dance, drama, art, craft, sculpture, pottery, digital art, photography etc. various artists and teachers mentor the children fostering their aesthetic, intellectual, physical and moral sensibilities, thus creating happier, balanced and well rounded lives.
