Admission Details

Admissions are open from Grade Play Group to PU II on first come first serve basis subject to availability of seats.

Admission Process

Meet the Admission Counsellor
Fill the admission enquiry form
Campus tour
Purchase and filling of application
Admission test
Discussion with the Principal / Vice Principal
On approval, payment of first term fees
Admission acceptance letter

Documents at the time of Admission

4 Photographs
Original Marks Card
Birth certificate (Xerox)
Official copy of report from previous school (Xerox)
Transfer certificate (original)
Caste & Income certificate (Xerox)
