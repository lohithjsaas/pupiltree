About us

Providing world class Education, at an affordable cost

Welcome to Pupil Tree School ICSE , A unique school with a spectacular academic record and traditions spanning over a decade. Our schools focus is to instill a strong sense of values and self-discipline in children. Our greatest strength lies in our commitment to a balanced education and continuous improvement.

Pupil Tree School was established in the year 2008 and is managed by Pupil Tree Foundation Trust. It is situated in the city of Ballari, Pupil Tree Foundation Trust also manages 3 Kindergartens, 1State board schools, and an ICSE Board school at Ballari. Currently, the institutions have a combined strength of 3000 students and 300 teaching and non-teaching staff.

Pupil Trees mission is to provide whole child education at an affordable cost that is well-balanced, engaging, innovative & creative which meets the needs and challenges of the 21st century.

Our Beliefs

Teaching & Learning

Pupil Tree strongly believes in learning everywhere, which does not confine to just classrooms.

We constantly upgrade our curriculum, upskill and train the teachers on the global best practices and teaching methodologies to think beyond traditional teaching methodologies like rote and chalk and talk. This enables teachers to make the class engaging and interactive.

Thus, giving an opportunity for every child to learn and understand the concepts taught in the way she/he best understands. This child-centric, collaborative approach and methodologies in teaching-learning make our children imaginative, curious, and confident.

Our Rules

Punctuality

Students are expected to be on time to School every day.

Discipline

Regular attendance and punctuality to School is emphasized.

Attire

Students are expected to come in their school uniform, neatly pressed and clean.

Restrictions

Wearing jewelry, mehendi and other accessories to school is not permitted.

Adornment

A pair of simple earrings, preferably studs may be worn to school every day.

Identification

All children are expected to wear the school ID card every day.

Responsibility

Care should be taken of the school property.

Consequences

Students are liable to make good all the damages they cause to school property.

Diary/Journal

School diaries should be brought to school every day.

Materials

Books must be brought to school according to the daily schedule.

Ownership

Students are responsible for labeling and securing their belongings. Lost items go to reception, but the school isn't responsible.

Prohibition

Mobile phones are strictly prohibited in school for students.

Accountability

Students found using mobile phones will be accountable for the consequences and the phone confiscated.

Authorization

No cameras, electronics, or clothing changes without specific permission from the class teacher.
